DDR Golf Classic

Welcome to the 4th Annual Dirty Downriver Golf Classic! This event is designed to bring together golf enthusiasts for a few days of fun, competition, and camaraderie. Whether you're a seasoned golfer or a beginner, there's something for everyone. Join us for a memorable experience filled with great golf, prizes, and community spirit.

Cost

The cost to participate in the Dirty Downriver Golf Classic is $140 per player. This fee includes:

  • Skins Entry into the tournament
  • CTP Entry into the tournament
  • Access to the awards ceremony
  • Custom DDR Swag
Players are responsible to cover their own greens fees, which will be paid directly to the golf courses. The cost to player each course will be:
  • Huntmore Golf Club: $78
  • West Shore Golf Club (Non-Members): $85

Registration is open now so please get them as soon as possible as we cannot hold spots for players. We need firm commits only. To register, please fill out the registration form available on our website.

Schedule

This year's schedule will be the following:

  • Friday, September 26th: Skins Game (TBD)
  • Saturday, September 27th: First Round at Huntmore Golf Club
  • Sunday, September 28th: Final Round at West Shore Golf Club
Players are encouraged to arrive early to warm up and familiarize themselves with the course.

The first round will begin at 10:00 AM on Saturday, with players teeing off in groups of four. The final round will take place on Sunday, with tee times starting approximately at 1:00 PM. The awards ceremony will follow the conclusion of the final round, where winners will be announced.

Format

The Dirty Downriver Golf Classic will feature a 2-round tournament format. Each round will consist of 18 holes, and players will compete in a 80% net individual competition. Each player must have an official USGA handicap to participate. The tournament will be played under the rules of the USGA, with local modifications as necessary.

The event will be held at Huntmore Golf Club in Brighton, Michigan, known for its challenging layout and beautiful scenery. The second round will be held at West Shore Golf Club in Grosse Ile, Michigan known for it The tournament will take place over two days, with the first round on Saturday and the final round on Sunday.

Skins will be held as well as CTPs for each round.
